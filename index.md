# Fabian Kutschera
## Introduction

I am currently attending the Masters Programme in Geophysics offered by LMU and TUM in Munich. My focus lies on seismology and tsunami genesis caused by coseismic seafloor displacement. I already combined both of these fields in my Bachelors Thesis, which covered earthquake-tsunami modelling in North Iceland, following a physics-based approach and using high performance computing. Extended results were presented at the virtual [EGU General Assembly 2021](https://meetingorganizer.copernicus.org/EGU21/EGU21-15891.html) and on-site at [EGU GA 2022](https://meetingorganizer.copernicus.org/EGU22/EGU22-13387.html).
In addition to the research field, I gained first experience within the reinsurance industry and supported the Cat Risk Management Team (R&D, GIS and Portfolio Analytics) at Allianz SE Reinsurance.
